About the Event

A faculty festival that celebrates mathematics, computing, innovation, creativity, and community spirit.

Math & Net Mashup Day 2026 is a faculty-wide programme designed to bring together students, staff, alumni, schools, and the wider community through meaningful and enjoyable activities.

It combines academic engagement, digital creativity, competition, networking, and fun in one shared campus experience. By creating a lively and inclusive environment, the event aims to make learning more engaging while strengthening relationships within and beyond the faculty.

! Objectives

  • Strengthen community spirit among students, staff, alumni, and external participants.
  • Encourage active learning through mathematics, computing, digital creativity, and innovation-based activities.
  • Provide opportunities for competition, talent development, and confident participation.
  • Promote networking, knowledge sharing, and exposure to real-world skills.
  • Create a fun, inclusive, and memorable faculty event experience.
